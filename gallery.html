Welcome to the Garden Gallery!

When you think of a traditional Chinese garden, you might think of rockeries, pavilions, lotus flowers, bridges over the water, and of course—the intricate lattice windows that frame the views. I've always admired the creative details of garden designs and the wisdom of the artisans behind them, and lattice windows are just the perfect example of this exquisite craftsmanship! Inspired by this timeless aesthetics, I want to create a “view-through-the-lattice” effect, as if you are walking past lattice windows and catching glimpses of the garden scenes beyond. The octagon-shaped images mimic the most distinctive lattice window frames. To createan immersive corridor-like feel, the gallery is horizontally scrollable. Take a stroll through the gallery, and enjoy these captured moments of garden beauty!
